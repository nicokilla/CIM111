THE COLLECTIVE

Southside Krooks is a South Florida underground collective of creatives. We have 8 members which consist of Recording Artist and Film markers , audio Engineers and produders. We have been working together for about 2 years.
"We strive to make the best quality of music but at the same time create sounds that have yet to be heard, its all about trying new things and explore youre creative abilities." - Nxghtime Producer SK
Within those 2 years we have been able to work with signed artist such as KapG , Nessly, Wifisfuneral and demotus.
