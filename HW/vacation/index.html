Força Barça


Barcelona, Spain


Soccer has been my passion ever since I remember. My whole life revolved around that as a child growning up Colombia. All I ever watched was Barcelona games and football matches. I quickly became addicted to it and then as I grew up studying Architecture and Design I learned about the vast history of Barcelona. The culture and ultimately the on going battle to separate and divorce from Spain.
Barcelona has always been a dream to visit.
Watching a soccer match and site seeing around "La Sagrada Familia" a church that has been undergoing contruction for years and will never be done due to the original structure of the design. Barcelona on top of my list of travel plans in my future.
